Context-Aware Road Accident Risk Prediction Using ANN

Ongoing

Addresses the global concern of road traffic accidents by developing a proactive risk assessment system. Uses Artificial Neural Networks to predict Low, Medium, and High accident risk levels based on contextual driving conditions like time of day, weather, road characteristics, and environmental factors.

Methodology includes feature engineering from temporal, environmental, and road-related attributes, data preprocessing with one-hot encoding, ANN training with ReLU activation and softmax output, and evaluation using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score. Trained on ~12,000 historical accident records from RTA Dataset.

TensorFlow • Scikit-learn • Pandas • Keras

Team: Dambewela D.M.T.D.B, Hangawaththa N.H.A.G.M, Sooriyaarachchi L.L.

VisionX – Smart Occupancy & Device Control

Ongoing

A vision-based intelligent automation system for large halls that detects people, estimates zone occupancy, and triggers device control decisions to improve energy efficiency. Uses a YOLO-based human detector with preprocessing, temporal smoothing, and hysteresis logic to avoid false triggers.

Key elements: real-time camera input (live or recorded), YOLO person detection, zone segmentation with centroid mapping, occupancy counting, and simulated IoT device control via a visual dashboard. Designed for scalability, stability, and minimal false activations in indoor environments.

Python • OpenCV • PyTorch • YOLO • IoT Simulation
